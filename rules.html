GAME RULES

PAY TEN OF YOUR FINEST ENGLISH POUNDS TO ENTER.

At the start of each gameweek, pick a team you think will win. You can only pick each team once throughout the competition, other than 2 pre-selected teams you may pick twice.

Points are awarded based on your selected team’s result according to the table below. The teams are split into quartiles based on league position at the start of the gameweek. Points reward bravery — away wins against higher quartile teams are worth more!

Also pick a player each week. They score points for any goals and assists. Use Haaland wisely!

The competition lasts until the end of the season. The player with the most points wins. What constitutes a gameweek is at the organiser’s discretion.

BE BRAVE — COWARDICE WILL GET YOU NOWHERE!
